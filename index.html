Upcoming Popup Meals

Hot Pot

Spicy soup base. People can add whatever they want to eat in the boiling soup, such as beef, chicken, vegetables, potatoes, etc. Then retrieve cooked food items from the pot and flavour them with individual dipping sauces.

Sichuan Spicy Chicken

Peppers used in Sichuan cuisines are very important. In this spicy chicken dish, we need to use bullet head chili and Sichuan peppercorn. It would make your tongue feel spicy and numbing.

Peking Duck

Beijing famous dish. The outside is very crispy. People can dip the meat in the sauce or make the duck wrap.

Chinese Dumpling

Dumplings consist of minced meat and finely chopped vegetables wrapped into a piece of dough skin. People can boil, fried, steam dumplings.

Chow mein

Chinese stir-fried noodles with vegetables and sometimes meat or tofu.
